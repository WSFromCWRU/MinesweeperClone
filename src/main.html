Minesweeper is about clicking grids and trying to avoid mines.
The first click will always reveal a blank area with numbers on the edges.
These numbers correspond to how many mines are in adjacent tiles.
If you right click a mine you put a flag there, marking that grid as a mine.

You win if all safe squares have been clicked!
